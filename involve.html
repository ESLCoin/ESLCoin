Governance and DAO

Participate in the DAO and get rewarded

ESL Coin TRX is designed to be an open sourced platform utility token. A DAO and an active community is expected take over the development and overall token governace.

Once phase 4 (CODENAME: TEACHER) is launched, there will be opportunities for token holders to participate in voting through staking their tokens.

Upon the conclusion of each vote, token holders with staked votes will receive rewards for participating in voting.

Token holders wishing to make a bigger impact may also apply to run for the ESL Coin Senate (1,000,000 ESL token minimum) where they may propose measures and receive greater rewards for voting.
